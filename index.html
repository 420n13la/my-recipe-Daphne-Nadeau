Breakfast crepes

Introduction

These crepes are easy to make and can be eaten with maple syrup or blueberry sauce and whipped cream. This recipe makes approximately 14 crepes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unbleached flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Softened butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, eggs, milk and vanilla extract, whisk until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat. When hot, brush with a little butter.
  3. For each crepe, pour about 3 tbsp of batter in the pan, tilt it to spread the batter evenly.
  4. When the crepe's edge peels off easily and begins to brown, flip it with a spatula
  5. Cook for another 10 seconds or until cooked through, and remove from pan.
  6. Place the crepes on a plate as they are finished
